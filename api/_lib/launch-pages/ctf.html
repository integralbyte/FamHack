Before You Start

Beginner-Focused CTF

This is a surface-level CTF designed to introduce beginners to the idea of capture the flag challenges.

Only first-year students are eligible to claim the prize, but students from other years are welcome to play as well.

The prize rule is there to support first years who are still settling in and trying this format for the first time.

SIGINT

This was just a surface-level demonstration of what a CTF can look like. If you want the deeper version, SIGINT is the University of Edinburgh security society that runs more of this kind of thing.

The website has a little extra for anyone nosy enough to open the console.
